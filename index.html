Virtual Lab provides cutting-edge IT technology for
material researchers.

Based on proven technology with many years of participation in national challenges, we provide material simulation platform Materials Square and
unlimited cloud delivery solution MAXFlops and cutting-edge services related to various simulations.

See More

MISSION

Our mission is to create services that users can easily simulate materials with minimal effort, and furthermore, to create “square of materials” gathering all information about materials around the world .

In addition, we are trying to activate the Korean material industry and to help those who want to start material simulation by sharing various information for free through a technology blog and running MatSQ forum.

Major National R&D Tasks

Virtual Lab plays a central role in various national projects related to material simulation.

- Development of web-based multi-scale simulation platform for efficient design of nanomaterials for energy, Korea Evaluation Institute of Industrial Technology, 2017

- Development of electronic research note KiRI Note for researchers, Korea Institute of Science and Technology, 2018

- Development of software for web platform application of nanomaterial computational simulation for electrodes, Korea Institute of Energy Research, 2018

- Development of web-based computational science platform framework SimPL, Ministry of Science and ICT, 2019 ~

- Machine learning based alloy powder design and manufacturing platform development using plasma high temperature reduction method, Korea Evaluation Institute of Industrial Technology, 2019 ~

- Researcher Behavior-based R & D Big Data Collection and Utilization Framework Development, Ministry of Science and ICT, 2019 ~
